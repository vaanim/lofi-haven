Lo-Fi Haven is built using a combination of JavaScript, HTML, and CSS, ensuring a seamless and interactive user experience. Throughout the development process, various resources such as Stack Overflow, W3Schools, and ChatGPT provided invaluable support, helping to troubleshoot issues, optimize performance, and refine the overall functionality of the platform.
